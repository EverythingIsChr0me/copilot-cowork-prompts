# Settlement Demand Letter

**Category:** `legal`

**Apps Involved:** Word, Outlook

**Complexity:** Moderate (3-4 apps)

### Description

Drafts a settlement demand letter using case facts, damages calculations, and legal theories from matter notes and documents.

### The Prompt

```
Using the case facts in [/CASE_SUMMARY] and the damages summary in [/DAMAGES_SUMMARY], draft a settlement demand letter addressed to [OPPOSING_PARTY_NAME], care of [OPPOSING_COUNSEL_NAME] at [OPPOSING_COUNSEL_EMAIL]. The letter should: (1) state the factual background concisely, (2) set out our legal theories of liability, (3) itemize economic and non-economic damages totaling [DEMAND_AMOUNT], (4) include a settlement deadline of [DEADLINE_DATE], and (5) close with a reservation of all rights. Use formal legal correspondence format. Save to [/MATTER_FOLDER/] and send a draft copy to [SUPERVISING_ATTORNEY_EMAIL] for approval before sending.
```

### Expected Outcome

A formal demand letter in Word, saved to SharePoint and routed to the supervising attorney for sign-off.

### Tips & Variations

- Do not send directly to opposing counsel — always route through a supervising attorney first.
- Variation: adjust tone for negotiation-friendly vs. litigation-ready posture by adding `use a firm but cooperative tone` or `use a firm litigation-ready tone`.
